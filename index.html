Anime is hand-drawn and computer-generated animation originating from Japan.
Outside of Japan and in English, anime refers specifically to animation produced in Japan.
However, in Japan and in Japanese, anime describes all animated works,
regardless of style or origin. From 2000 to 2010, an average of 209 anime were produced
per year and in the last decade; from 2010 to 2020, an average of 300 anime were produced per year
